Course Outline

Part 1: Introduction: Structural Design Process; Structural System Requirements, Building Codes and Design Standards

Part 2: Gravity Load Systems: Basic Floor and Roof Systems, One- and two-way bending, Tributary Areas for Floor and Roof Loads; One-Way Floor Systems, Two-Way Floor Systems, Span Lengths and Design Issues Lateral Load Systems: Basic Lateral Load Systems, Moment Frames, Braced Frames, Shear Walls and Horizontal Diaphragms Structural Design Concepts: Limit States Design, Structural Safety, Strength Limit State, Serviceability, Durability/Sustainability

Part 3: Gravity Loads for Buildings: Evaluation of Dead Load and Live Load, Load Combinations

Part 4: Introduction to Structural Steel: Material Properties and Behaviour of Structural Steel; Failure Modes, Local Buckling, Cross-section Classifications, Flexural Buckling, Effective Length, Compressive Resistance of W-shape Columns, Flexural Resistance of Laterally Supported W-shape Beams, Shear resistance of W-shape beams

Part 5:Introduction to Structural Concrete: Material Properties and Behaviour of Structural Concrete, Flexural Analysis of Beams, Assumptions and general behaviour, Failure modes, Analysis at failure, Cracking moment and minimum reinforcement, One-way slabs, Flexural Design of Beams, Singly-reinforced sections
