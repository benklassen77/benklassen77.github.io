Attributes

Attribute Function
Strength Melee damage dealt and likelihood of landing a hit
Dexterity Ranged damage dealt and likelihood of hit plus defense
Constitution Vitality
Wisdom Jedi force powers and saving throws
Charisma Affects how many force points used and persuade
Intelligence How many skill points attained per level

Combat calculations

Attack modifier only affects whether or not hit is landed. Does not factor into damage calculation

Attack roll = D20 + Attack Modifier + Str/Dex Modifier

  • Chance to hit
  • D20 is roll on a 20 sided die. If 1 rolled, automatic miss. If 20 rolled, automatic hit
  • Strength melee, dex blaster
  • in Kotor 1, dex/str used for lightsaber, whichever is higher. However, strength still required for damage

Defense roll: 10 + Defense + Dex modifier

  • if attacker number is equal to or greater than defense number, hit occurs

Saving throws

3 kinds of saves: reflex, fortitude, and will

Reflex: saving against mines and grenades

Fortitude: saving against poison

Will: saving against force powers

  • effect trying to resist has DC, or difficulty class. You need to match or exceed this to resist the effect

Saving throw = D20 + save + attribute modifier

  • Reflex associated with dexterity
  • Fortitude associated with constitution
  • Will associated with wisdom

Skills

Skill check = D20 + skill rank + attribute modifier

  • Demolitions associated with intelligence
  • Stealth associated with awareness and intelligence as attribute
  • Persuasion uses charisma as governing attribute

Critical hits

  • if you roll a 20 (weapon will specify), you get to roll one more time for a critical hit
  • if attack is greater than defense in critical roll, damage may be doubled
  • critical damage not affected by stealth at all. Attacking from stealth only means you attack first. However, if you are scoundrel, you can do extra damage with sneak attack

Debunk

  • double-bladed lightsabre uses 2-weapon fighting
  • rifles use neither 2-weapon or dueling
  • double-bladed lightsabre is equally likely to hit as short and regular sabre. If not using double, use a short in off-hand and a regular in normal hand
    • double can do more damage because both attack rolls are based off one weapon. If doing small lightsabre, it will deal less damage
    • if you use 2 lightsabres, you can double your attributes

Damage Attack

Damage roll = Dice roll(s) + attribute modifier + bonuses (feats, upgrades)

  • Eg. longsword is 1-12. This means damage is D12 plus strength modifier
  • Double sword has 2 rolls of a 6-sided die. If 2-12, this means a 2 or a 12 has a 1/36 chance
  • Vulkar shock stick, 2-5, means 1 free damage plus roll on 4 sided die

Damage Reduction

  • if 5/- vs slashing, you ignore 5 points of damage from slashing

Damage per weapon

  • blades deal piercing damage
  • stun sticks deal bludgeoning damage
  • swords deal slashing
  • blasters deal energy
  • droids deal fire, cold, and electric
  • sith deal mind-affecting and electric

Force powers

Heal: 10 vitality + 1 point per charisma and wisdom mod and level of force user

Force Armour: + 6 defense bonus and all saving throws. Great for jedi with low dexterity

Master Speed: defense increases to 4 and 2 extra attacks per round

Mastor Valor: all members gain 5 points for all attributes plus saving throws. Strength, dexterity, wisdom etc.

Force Immunity: great if you have low wisdom

Plague: no resistance, 12 points per physical attribute

Force Breach: only good against light side jedi
